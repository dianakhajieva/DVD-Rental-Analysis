## Project Overview
This project presents an **end-to-end analytics dashboard** built to analyze a movie rental business across multiple dimensions, including **customers, revenue, films, geography, and store performance**.

The dashboard is designed with a **business-first approach**, focusing on actionable insights, clean visuals, and intuitive navigation.  
It is suitable for **executive reporting, operational monitoring, and data analyst portfolios**.

## Dashboard Structure

### Customer Analysis
This page focuses on **customer behavior, engagement, and loyalty**. It provides insights into how actively customers use the platform, how frequently they rent, and overall retention strength. The analysis helps identify highly engaged customers and understand usage patterns across the customer base.

---

### Financial Trends
The Financial Trends page highlights **revenue performance and payment activity over time**. It enables stakeholders to track overall financial health, identify revenue trends, and understand how different content categories contribute to total earnings.

---

### Film & Actor Analysis
This section analyzes **content performance and talent impact**. It compares rental demand against revenue generation across films, genres, and actors, helping support decisions related to content acquisition, pricing, and catalog optimization.

---

### Geographic Trends
The Geographic Trends page examines **rental and revenue distribution across regions**. It helps identify high-performing countries and cities, uncover regional demand patterns, and highlight opportunities for market expansion or targeted strategies.

---

### Staff & Store Performance
This page evaluates **operational efficiency at the store and staff level**. It compares performance metrics across locations and employees, supporting productivity analysis and operational optimization.

---

## Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Track customer engagement and retention
- Identify top-performing films, actors, and categories
- Monitor financial trends and revenue drivers
- Optimize store and staff performance
- Make data-driven strategic decisions
